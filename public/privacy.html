Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Quick Summary: We collect only the information needed to organize pickleball games (your name and phone number). We NEVER share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We use your phone number solely to send you game confirmations and updates via SMS.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use PicklePlay Scheduler, we collect:

  • Name: To identify you in game registrations
  • Phone Number: To send SMS confirmations and game updates
  • Game Preferences: Games you create or join

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address (for security)
  • Local Storage: Game management links stored in your browser

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Game Organization: Display your name in player lists
  • SMS Notifications: Send game confirmations, updates, and cancellations
  • Waitlist Management: Notify you when spots become available
  • Game Management: Allow organizers to manage their games

Communication Purposes

We send SMS messages for:

  • Game confirmation when you join
  • Promotion from waitlist to confirmed player
  • Game updates (time changes, location changes)
  • Game cancellation notifications
  • Waitlist notifications

3. SMS Messaging Policy

We will NEVER share, sell, or distribute your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in data to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS Consent and Opt-Out

  • Opt-In: You consent to SMS when providing your phone number during game registration
  • Frequency: Message frequency varies (typically 1-3 messages per game)
  • Charges: Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile plan
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to immediately opt-out
  • Help: Reply HELP for support information

SMS Data Security

Your phone number is:

  • Stored securely and used only for game-related notifications
  • Never shared with other players or third parties
  • Automatically deleted when you cancel your registration
  • Protected by industry-standard security measures

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

What We Share

We only share your information in these limited circumstances:

  • Game Participants: Your name is visible to other players in the same game
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With Twilio (our SMS provider) to send messages

What We DON'T Share

  • Your phone number with other players
  • Your information for marketing purposes
  • Your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Personal information with social media companies

5. Data Storage and Security

How We Protect Your Data

  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information
  • Data Minimization: We collect only what's necessary
  • Regular Cleanup: Old game data is periodically removed

Data Retention

  • Active Games: Data retained while game is active
  • Completed Games: Data may be retained for up to 90 days
  • Cancelled Registration: Your data is immediately removed
  • SMS Opt-Out: Phone number removed from all future messaging

6. Your Rights and Choices

You Can:

  • Cancel Registration: Remove yourself from any game at any time
  • Opt-Out of SMS: Reply STOP to any message
  • Request Data Deletion: Contact us to delete your information
  • Update Information: Contact us to correct your information

Browser Data

We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember games you've created (management links)
  • Improve your user experience
  • You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings

7. Children's Privacy

PicklePlay Scheduler is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • We'll notify users of significant changes via SMS or email
  • Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes

9. Third-Party Services

Twilio (SMS Provider)

We use Twilio to send SMS messages. Twilio's privacy policy applies to how they handle SMS delivery. Your phone number is shared with Twilio solely for message delivery purposes.

Google Calendar Integration

When you use "Add to Calendar," you're redirected to Google Calendar. Google's privacy policy applies to any information you share with Google Calendar.

10. Contact Us

Privacy Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information:

  • Email: privacy@pickleplay.club
  • SMS Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message
  • General Support: support@pickleplay.club

We respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours.

11. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process your information based on:

  • Consent: You explicitly consent to SMS messaging
  • Legitimate Interest: Game organization and communication
  • Contract Performance: Providing the game scheduling service
