How Our SMS Consent Process Works

This page demonstrates exactly how players consent to receive SMS notifications when joining pickleball games through our platform.

Player Registration Flow

When players click on a game link shared by organizers, they see this registration form:

SMS Consent Process Steps

1 Player provides phone number - Required field in registration form
2 Player checks consent box - Cannot submit without agreeing to SMS notifications
3 Player clicks "Count Me In!" - Explicit action to join and consent to SMS
4 Automatic SMS confirmation sent - Immediate confirmation of registration

Sample SMS Messages We Send

Confirmation Message:

"You're confirmed for Pickleball at Oak Park Courts on Sat, Jan 25 at 2:00 PM! You are Player 3 of 8. Reply 9 to cancel."

Waitlist Message:

"You've been added to the waitlist for Pickleball at Oak Park Courts. You are #2 on the waitlist. We'll notify you if a spot opens up! Reply 9 to cancel."

Update Message:

"UPDATE: Your pickleball game at Oak Park Courts has been moved to 3:00 PM same date. All other details remain the same."

Cancellation Message:

"CANCELLED: Your pickleball game at Oak Park Courts on Sat, Jan 25 has been cancelled. Reason: Weather conditions."

Privacy & Data Protection

  • No Third-Party Sharing: Phone numbers are NEVER shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes
  • Purpose-Limited: SMS only used for game-related notifications the user signed up for
  • Easy Opt-Out: Users can reply STOP to any message to immediately opt-out
  • Support Available: Users can reply HELP for assistance
  • Transparent: All messaging practices disclosed during signup

Contact Information:

For questions about SMS notifications or to opt-out: Reply STOP to any message or email support@pickleplay.club
